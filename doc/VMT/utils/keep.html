keep

PURPOSE

KEEP keeps the caller workspace variables of your choice and clear the rest.

SYNOPSIS

function keep(varargin);

DESCRIPTION 

KEEP keeps the caller workspace variables of your choice and clear the rest.
       Its usage is just like "clear" but only for variables.
